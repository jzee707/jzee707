VACCINE REGISTRATION


The LGU of General Santos City has set up this online platform for registration to our vaccination campaign. Once you sign up, we will validate the information you have provided and inform you of your schedule and appointment details through your institution, barangay, association, registered cellphone number and/or email address provided in your registration.

For persons with comorbidities listed under the priority groups(A3), please include also the contact details of your physician in the uploaded files for online or telephone verification.

There will be NO WALK-INs at the Vaccination Site, We encourage you to pre-register in order to be included in the allocation and printing of vaccination cards.
