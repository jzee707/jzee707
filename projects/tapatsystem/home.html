Trace And Protect Action Team (TAPAT) System

is designed to alert the registrant and the household in cases where a possible contact or encounter happened at a certain site and date/time to a COVID19 Suspect/Probable/Confirmed resident or traveller.

Residents of General Santos City should use their color-coded Electronic Quarantine Pass (EQP) previously issued by their respective barangays last March. TAPAT Registration is not required for EQP holders, and qualified members of the same household, unless requested by competent health professionals.

REGISTRATION

Non-resident of GenSan who will enter the City of GenSan should register their QR ID like CCTS, MDTC, Cotabato QR to TAPAT for Contact Tracing and Health Monitoring purposes and for those people who doesn’t have Contact Tracing IDs they can Generate TAPAT QR instead. The registered QR ID in TAPAT will be used for scanning upon entering to any border checkpoints, establishments and offices.

TAPAT QR SCANNER

A customized QR reader developed by LGU-GENSAN intended to replace the usual logbook system in entering any establishment, government offices and checkpoints and it enables LGU-Health Official to conduct contact tracing way faster than the current manual contact tracing.
