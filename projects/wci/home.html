Western Colleges High School Department

Philosophy

"A sound mind in a sound body".This gives a well-rounded life as the ideal forward which every student.

Vision

Produce productive, holistic and competent who can by examples.

Mission

Provide quality education imbued with Christian values and be globally competitive.

Objectives

01
Give quality education to all individuals.
02
Develop individuals, physically, mentally, spiritually and moral upright.
03
Develop self-directed individuals who can lead through active participation at home, school and in the community.
04
Encourage the dynamic participation of faculty and students in socio-civic activities to alleviate the flight of less privileged members of the community.
05
Provide global education thru the establishment of linkages/affiliation with government and non-government institutions/agencies here and abroad.
